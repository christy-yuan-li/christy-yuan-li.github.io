Ph.D. candidate at Duke University, advised by Professor Lawrence Carin. Research interests lie in NLP, AutoML, and scalable ML.

Research Interests

  • AutoML: automatic synchronization strategy search in data-parallel distributed deep learning.
  • Pre-training and self-supervised learning for language understanding and generation.
  • Graph structure understanding via Graph Transformers.
  • NLP in medical diagnosis (e.g., medical image report generation, medical diagnosis and discharge medication recomendation).
  • Medical visual diagnosis (e.g., chest x-ray image diagnoses localization and organ segmentation).
